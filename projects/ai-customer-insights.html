AI Driven Customer Insights Analysis

Event-driven pipeline that turns recurring support signals into root-cause insights — faster triage, clearer patterns, and a repeatable workflow.

AWS Lambda SQS RAG DynamoDB Gemini / LLM

The Challenge

Teams were manually reviewing thousands of support interactions to find patterns. The process was slow, inconsistent, and hard to scale — and critical issues were often discovered late.

The Solution

Built an event-driven pipeline to ingest signals, normalize text, and generate structured insights. Added a RAG-based compliance layer to validate responses against policy docs and produce consistent outputs.

⚡ The Impact

Reduced manual analysis and made root-cause trends visible faster — enabling quicker decisions, better prioritization, and repeatable reporting.
